Why Dachshunds are the best dogs

There are hundreds of dog breeds out there, but in my opinion no dog holds a candle to the dachshund. Also known as a wiener dog, this breed has it all. It’s cute, has a massive personality, and a loyal heart. Here's why I'm convinced dachshunds are the best dogs on the planet.

Their silhouette is iconic

Let's start with the obvious. Dachshunds look like no other animal on Earth. With their short legs, long barrel-shaped bodies, floppy ears, and expressive eyes, they are walking works of art. They come in three coat varieties (smooth, wirehaired, and longhaired) and two sizes (standard and miniature), so there's a perfect dachshund for every kind of person.

Their personalities

Don't let their small stature fool you, dachshunds have all the personality of a large dog. Dachshunds were originally bred in Germany to hunt badgers, and that history gave them a bold, fearless, and tenacious spirit that still shows up every single day… even if it’s just to bark at the FedEx delivery man. They are incredibly smart, endlessly curious, and famously stubborn. Living with a dachshund is like living with a tiny, furry roommate who has strong opinions about everything. My dachshund, named Neville Longbottom, frequently growls to tell me what he thinks of my actions. And while he might give me an egregious amount of side eye, we all know I’m his favorite person and he’ll stick by my side (or on my lap) all day long.

They Make Every Day More Fun

There is never a dull moment with a wiener dog around. The way they run and hop like bunnies, the big bark that echoes out of their body, and the dramatic look of betrayal when you eat cheese without sharing are endlessly entertaining. They are also naturally photogenic, which is why dachshund accounts (including mine) dominate social media. No matter what they're doing, they look incredible.Once a dachshund has your heart, there's no going back. And I’d have it no other way. If you get a dachshund, please just make sure you get a ramp and support their back. They are the best, but they aren’t invincible.
